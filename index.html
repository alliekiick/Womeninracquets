Our Mission

WomenInRacquets.com is more than a network — it’s a movement. A community built to empower, elevate, and unite women leaders across racquet sports and hospitality. Here, leadership meets connection, education meets action, and women thrive.

Why We’re Unique

  • The only curated network for women directors in racquet sports
  • Led by experienced professionals, grounded in purpose
  • Connection over competition: mentoring, resources, and real relationships
  • Private and powerful: a space to be bold, vulnerable, and visionary

About the Founder

Allie Kiick is a former professional tennis player now forging a path in racquet leadership. With a career shaped by both triumph and perseverance, Allie brings heart, hustle, and high standards to this new frontier — creating the space she wished existed all along.
